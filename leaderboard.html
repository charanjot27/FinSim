Scale on the leaderboard

FinSim ships with a Firestore-backed global leaderboard. Sign in with Google inside the game, trade a few sessions, and your row will appear alongside live players and twenty seeded bots. Your equity and P&L update live with a 5-second debounce and a 30-second heartbeat — so you stay on the board even while you’re idle.
